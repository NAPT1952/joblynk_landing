Why Germany Needs Indian Hotel Staff

Germany has labor shortage after Covid. Hotels in Berlin, Munich, Frankfurt hiring Indians for kitchen, F&B, housekeeping. No degree needed for cook/waiter roles.

Germany Work Visa Requirements

  1. Job Offer: From German hotel with salary €2,500+ for Skilled Worker Visa
  2. Experience: 2 years in hotels OR IHM diploma
  3. German Language: B1 level from Goethe Institute. A1 enough for kitchen jobs
  4. Age: Under 45 preferred
  5. Clean record: PCC + medical

How to Apply Step-by-Step

  1. Learn German: 6 months to B1. Cost ₹30k-50k
  2. Get experience: 2 years in 3/4/5-star hotel India
  3. Apply: Via Make it in Germany portal, Indeed.de, or agencies
  4. Interview: On Zoom. Speak basic German
  5. Contract: Get job offer letter
  6. Visa: Apply at VFS Germany. Processing 4-8 weeks. Fee €75

Agencies Hiring for Germany

  • TalentBeyondBoundaries: Free, for refugees/skilled workers
  • Careers International: Mumbai based, ₹50k fee after visa
  • MME India: Delhi, charges ₹1.5L total
  • Warning: Never pay ₹3L+ upfront. Real agencies take money after visa.
