The Story Behind JobLynk.live

A hospitality executive who knew the industry's biggest problem — and built the solution professionals actually needed.

Our Story

Not an Agency. An Insider.

JobLynk.live was born from a simple frustration — Indian hospitality professionals were talented, qualified, and hardworking, but had no real tools to get hired faster.

Job boards were cluttered with outdated listings. Recruitment agencies took large commissions. Resume templates were generic. And nobody was giving professionals the one thing they really needed — direct access to the right people.

So Nigel Thomas — a hospitality industry executive with years of hands-on experience across hotels, cruise lines, and maritime operations — built JobLynk.live as the platform he wished had existed when he was building his own career.

The result: A lean, powerful career toolkit that gives professionals a polished resume, verified HR contacts, and daily fresh leads — all for less than a cup of coffee.
