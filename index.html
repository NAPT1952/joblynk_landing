📞 BPO / Call Center Jobs

BPO jobs are one of the most popular non-technical career options, especially for freshers. They provide quick entry into the job market with fast hiring cycles.

Common Roles

  • Customer Support Executive - Handle inbound calls and resolve customer queries
  • Telecaller - Outbound sales and lead generation calls
  • Voice Process Agent - International/domestic voice support
  • Non-Voice Support - Email, chat, and back-office processes

Skills Required

  • Good communication skills in English + regional language
  • Active listening ability
  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Patience and problem-solving attitude

Why Choose BPO?

BPO jobs offer flexible shifts, fast hiring within 1-7 days, and clear growth paths to Team Leader, Quality Analyst, and Operations Manager roles. Many MNCs hire freshers with just 12th pass or graduate qualifications.

Where to Apply in Mumbai

Top BPO companies: TCS, Wipro, Accenture, Concentrix, Tech Mahindra, Teleperformance. Also check outsourcing firms and customer service centers in Andheri, Malad, and Navi Mumbai.
