🧹 Housekeeping Admin

Complete Career Guide – Hotels, Resorts, Cruise Lines

Housekeeping Career Roles & Salaries (Annual INR)

  • Housekeeping Associate: ₹2 – 3 LPA
  • Housekeeping Supervisor: ₹3 – 5 LPA
  • Floor Manager: ₹4 – 7 LPA
  • Housekeeping Manager: ₹5 – 10 LPA
  • Executive Housekeeper (Luxury): ₹8 – 15 LPA
  • Director of Housekeeping (International): ₹15 – 25 LPA

Room Cleaning Standards

  • Standard room turnaround: 25 minutes
  • Deep cleaning: 45 minutes
  • VIP room preparation: 15 minutes with special amenities
  • Public area cleaning: Continuous throughout shift

Key KPIs for Housekeeping

  • Room Inspection Score: Target 95%+
  • Turnaround Time: Target under 30 min
  • Guest Cleanliness Rating: Target 4.8/5
  • Lost & Found Resolution Rate: 100% logged and tracked
  • Linen Inventory Accuracy: 99%+ variance tolerance

Required Certifications

  • Infection Control Certification
  • HACCP / Food Safety for F&B areas
  • Green Cleaning Certification
  • Laundry Management Certification
