⚓ Cruise and Maritime Certifications

STCW & Maritime Training

Mandatory certifications for cruise ship and merchant navy jobs. Know exactly what you need, where to get it, and how much it costs.

🚨

STCW Basic Safety Training (BST)

Mandatory for ALL cruise ship and maritime jobs. Covers fire fighting, survival craft, first aid, personal safety.

⏱️ 4–5 days💰 ₹8,000–15,000📅 Valid 5 years
⚓

Competency Certificate (CoC)

Required for deck and engine officers on merchant navy vessels. Issued by DG Shipping India.

⏱️ Exam based💰 ₹2,000–5,000 fees📅 5 year validity
🏥

Medical Fitness Certificate (ENG1)

⏱️ 1 day💰 ₹3,000–6,000📅 2 year validity
🛟

Proficiency in Survival Craft (PSC)

Required to work as a Lifeboat Commander on cruise ships and vessels.

⏱️ 5 days💰 ₹12,000–18,000
🍽️

Food Safety Certificate (HACCP)

Required for all galley / kitchen staff on cruise ships. International food safety standard.

⏱️ 1–2 days💰 ₹2,000–4,000
📍

Where to Get STCW in Mumbai

Anglo Eastern Maritime Academy, Lal Bahadur Shastri College of Advanced Maritime Studies, Maritime Training Institute Navi Mumbai.

📍 Navi Mumbai / Andheri

📞 BPO and Call Center Training

Get BPO Ready Fast

What BPO companies actually train you on — and where to get ahead before joining.

🗣️

Spoken English and Accent Training

Most BPOs provide in-house training. Also available at British Council Mumbai and local institutes in Andheri, Dadar.

💰 ₹3,000–8,000⏱️ 1–3 months
💬

Customer Service Certification

HDI, ITIL or company-specific customer service certifications valued for team leader and quality roles.

💰 ₹5,000–15,000⏱️ Online or classroom
⌨️

Typing Speed and MS Office

Non-voice BPO roles require 30–50 WPM typing speed. Free practice on TypingClub or Ratatype online.

💰 Free online⏱️ Self-paced
