🚚 Operations & Supply Chain Careers in India 2026

📅 May 2026 ⏱ 11 min read 🏷️ #OperationsJobs

India’s logistics and supply chain sector is undergoing a massive transformation, driven by e-commerce giants (Amazon, Flipkart), quick-commerce (Blinkit, Zepto), and manufacturing push (PLI schemes). Operations professionals ensure goods move efficiently, costs are controlled, and customers are satisfied.

Key roles in operations & supply chain

  • Warehouse Operations Manager: Oversee inventory, staffing. Salary: 5–12 LPA.
  • Logistics Coordinator: Plan routes, track shipments. 3.5–8 LPA.
  • Procurement Specialist: Source raw materials. 5–10 LPA.
  • Last-Mile Delivery Manager: Manage delivery partners. 6–14 LPA.
💡 Pro tip: Operations roles offer rapid growth – many plant heads and regional ops directors earn 25–40 LPA.

How to get hired

Target Amazon India, Flipkart, or manufacturers like Tata, Mahindra. Freshers start as operations executives (3–5 LPA) and can double salary in 3 years.
