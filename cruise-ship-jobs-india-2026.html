Documents Required for Cruise Jobs

  1. STCW 2010: Basic Safety Training. 10 days course. Cost: ₹12k-18k
  2. Passport: Valid 2+ years
  3. Seaman Book: CDC from DG Shipping India
  4. Medical: DGS approved medical test
  5. US C1/D Visa: Company sponsors after selection
  6. Police Clearance: PCC from passport office

How to Apply With No Experience

  1. Hotel experience helps: 6 months in any hotel/restaurant
  2. Apply via agencies: AirBorne Recruit, Kamaxi Overseas, Girik Maritime - DG Shipping approved
  3. Direct: Royal Caribbean Careers, Carnival Careers websites
  4. Avoid agents asking for money: Real agencies don't charge before joining

Top Cruise Lines Hiring Indians

  • Royal Caribbean: 5000+ Indians. Best for first timers
  • Carnival Cruise Line: High tips for F&B staff
  • MSC Cruises: Hiring from Mumbai, Delhi, Goa
  • Norwegian Cruise Line: Good for housekeeping
Pro Tip: Learn basic cocktail making + English speaking. Interview rounds are tough. Practice "Tell me about yourself" for 2 mins.
