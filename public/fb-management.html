Complete Career Guide for Indian Professionals

Overview

This sector offers excellent career opportunities for Indian hospitality professionals. Salary ranges from ₹3 LPA to ₹20 LPA depending on experience and role.

Key Roles

  • Entry Level: ₹2.5 – 4 LPA
  • Supervisory: ₹4 – 7 LPA
  • Managerial: ₹8 – 15 LPA
  • Executive: ₹18 – 40 LPA

Top Employers in India

  • Taj Hotels, Oberoi, ITC, Marriott, Hyatt, Accor, Hilton
  • Specialty restaurants and QSR chains
  • Cruise lines and maritime companies

How to Apply

  • Update your CV with relevant experience
  • Apply directly on hotel career portals
  • Network on LinkedIn with HR managers
  • Prepare for behavioral interviews
