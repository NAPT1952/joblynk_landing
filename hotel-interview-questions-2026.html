Documents Required for Cruise Jobs

  1. STCW 2010: Basic Safety Training. 10 days course. Cost: ₹12k-18k
  2. Passport: Valid 2+ years
  3. Seaman Book: CDC from DG Shipping India
  4. Medical: DGS approved medical test
  5. US C1/D Visa: Company sponsors after selection
  6. Police Clearance: PCC from passport office

How to Apply With No Experience

  1. Hotel experience helps: 6 months in any hotel/restaurant
  2. Apply via agencies: AirBorne Recruit, Kamaxi Overseas, Girik Maritime - DG Shipping approved
  3. Direct: Royal Caribbean Careers, Carnival Careers websites
  4. Avoid agents asking for money: Real agencies don't charge before joining

Top Cruise Lines Hiring Indians

  • Royal Caribbean: 5000+ Indians. Best for first timers
  • Carnival Cruise Line: High tips for F&B staff
  • MSC Cruises: Hiring from Mumbai, Delhi, Goa
  • Norwegian Cruise Line: Good for housekeeping
Pro Tip: Learn basic cocktail making + English speaking. Interview rounds are tough. Practice "Tell me about yourself" for 2 mins.

50 Hotel Interview Questions & Answers 2026

Updated: May 6, 2026

STAR Method: Situation → Task → Action → Result. Use this to answer behavioral questions. HR managers love it.

Common Questions For All Hotel Jobs

Q1. Tell me about yourself.
A: "I'm a hospitality professional with 2 years in F&B service. At Taj Mumbai, I handled 80+ guests daily, increased upselling by 15%. I'm passionate about guest service and want to grow with a 5-star brand like yours."
Q2. Why do you want to work in hotels?
A: "I love meeting people from different cultures. Hotels teach discipline, grooming, and communication. I want a career where I can grow from trainee to manager in 5 years."
Q3. How do you handle angry guests? STAR Example
A: "Situation: Guest at ITC shouted about late room service. Task: Calm him + solve issue. Action: I apologized, offered complimentary dessert, checked with kitchen personally. Result: Guest calmed down, gave 5-star review, tipped ₹500."

Front Office Interview Questions

Q4. What is your check-in process?
A: "Greet → Verify ID + booking → Collect payment/deposit → Explain amenities → Issue key → Wish pleasant stay. Total 3 mins max."
Q5. Guest has no reservation but hotel is full. What do you do?
A: "Apologize sincerely. Check nearby partner hotels. Call and book for guest. Offer taxi. Follow up next day. Guest remembers help more than no-room."

F&B Service Interview Questions

Q6. How do you upsell?
A: "Suggest, don't push. 'Sir, our chef special goes well with this wine. Shall I get you a tasting portion?' Upselling = 20% of my monthly incentive at last job."
Q7. Guest finds hair in food. What now?
A: "1. Apologize immediately 2. Remove dish 3. Inform manager 4. Offer fresh dish + complimentary dessert 5. Never argue. Guest safety first."

Housekeeping Interview Questions

Q8. How many rooms can you clean in 8 hours?
A: "14-16 rooms with 5-star standard. Checkout rooms take 30 mins, stayover 15 mins. Quality over speed."

Tricky HR Questions

Q9. Why should we hire you with no experience?
A: "I'm fresher but I'm trainable, punctual, and hungry to learn. I did 3 months training at IHM. I'll work double shifts if needed. Experienced people have habits, I have no bad habits."
Q10. What are your salary expectations?
A: "As per industry standard for this role. I'm more focused on learning and growth. If you can share the range, I can confirm."
Final Tip: Reach 10 mins early. Grooming = 50% marks. Black shoes, no beard, smile. Carry 2 copies resume + pen.
