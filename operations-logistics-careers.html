🚚 Operations & Supply Chain Careers in India 2026

India’s logistics and supply chain sector is undergoing a massive transformation, driven by e-commerce giants like Amazon, Flipkart, and quick-commerce like Blinkit.

Key roles in operations & supply chain

  • Warehouse Operations Manager: Salary: 5–12 LPA.
  • Logistics Coordinator: Salary: 3.5–8 LPA.
  • Procurement Specialist: Salary: 5–10 LPA.
  • Last-Mile Delivery Manager: Salary: 6–14 LPA.

Target Amazon India, Flipkart, or manufacturers like Tata. Freshers start at 3–5 LPA and can double salary in 3 years.
