🍽️ F&B Management

Complete Career Guide – Restaurants, Hotels, QSR, Cloud Kitchens

Types of F&B Establishments Hiring Managers

  • Hotel F&B Outlets – Fine dining, specialty restaurants, banquets
  • Standalone Restaurants – Fine dining, casual dining, themed
  • QSR Chains – McDonald's, KFC, Pizza Hut, Domino's, Burger King
  • Cloud Kitchens – Rebel Foods, Curefoods, EatFit, Box8
  • Coffee Chains – Starbucks, Cafe Coffee Day, Third Wave Coffee

F&B Management Roles & Salaries (Annual INR)

  • Restaurant Manager (Casual): ₹4 – 8 LPA
  • Restaurant Manager (Fine Dining): ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • F&B Controller: ₹5 – 10 LPA
  • Banquet Manager: ₹6 – 12 LPA
  • Beverage Manager: ₹5 – 10 LPA
  • Multi-Unit Manager: ₹10 – 18 LPA
  • Regional Operations Manager: ₹15 – 30 LPA
  • Director of F&B (5-star): ₹20 – 40 LPA

Key Skills for F&B Managers

  • P&L management and cost control
  • Inventory management and vendor negotiation
  • Staff training and development
  • Menu engineering and pricing strategy
  • Food safety compliance (FSSAI, HACCP, ServSafe)
  • POS system expertise (Micros, Toast, Petpooja)
📌 Pro Tip: ServSafe certification can increase your starting salary by ₹1-2 LPA.

Top F&B Companies Hiring in India

  • Barbeque Nation – 150+ outlets
  • Specialty Restaurants – 150+ outlets
  • Jubilant FoodWorks (Domino's) – 1,800+ stores
  • Devyani International (KFC, Pizza Hut) – 1,200+ stores
  • Rebel Foods – 400+ cloud kitchens
  • Tata Starbucks – 350+ stores
